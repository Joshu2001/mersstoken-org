What is Merss?

Merss (MSZ) is a decentralized crypto token designed to make lending, borrowing, and earning rewards simple and accessible for everyone. There are no banks involved, no middlemen, and no complicated setup required.

By locking your Merss tokens, you can earn up to 10% in rewards over time. You can even transfer those rewards to others without giving up control of your tokens. This feature is known as delegated yield.

Fast, secure, and environmentally friendly, Merss is built for the bold, those creating wealth, shaping the future, and joining a movement greater than themselves.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I need to withdraw early? ▼
Merss is designed for flexibility. While longer stakes offer higher rewards, you can withdraw your principal and accrued yield at any time, though early withdrawals might forfeit some bonus yield depending on your chosen Pod.
Is this staking or lending? ▼
Merss offers a unique hybrid. You stake your tokens to earn yield, but the protocol also facilitates secure lending and borrowing markets where your staked tokens can be used as collateral without ever leaving your control.
How do I know my funds are safe? ▼
Security is paramount. Merss undergoes rigorous smart contract audits, and your funds remains non-custodial. We prioritize transparency and have built-in safeguards to protect your assets.

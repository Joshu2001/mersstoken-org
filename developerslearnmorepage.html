Developers: Build on the Trust Economy

Integrate. Innovate. Empower trust at the protocol level.

Merss isn’t just another token—it’s a programmable trust layer for the next generation of decentralized apps. As a developer, you can extend its capabilities, contribute to the ecosystem, or integrate it into your own dApps, DAOs, or platforms.

What You Can Build With Merss

  • Yield-based dApps: Use Proof of Trust Epochs (PoTE) to reward long-term behavior instead of activity farming.
  • Composable Finance Protocols: Leverage Delegated Yield Ledger (DYL) to enable:
    • Trust-based lending and borrowing
    • Yield streaming and programmable repayments
    • Micro-sponsorships and DAO support flows
  • Trust-weighted Governance Systems: Build DAOs or voting modules that factor time held, not just raw token weight.
  • Creator Economy Tools: Create plugins for platforms like Regaarder where fans pledge Mites (micro-units) to creators without financial transactions.
  • Reputation-based Credit Models: Integrate Merss trust epochs into on-chain identity systems to form epoch-weighted credit scores.

Example Use Cases to Explore

Auto-streaming loan repayments via Mites

Automate loan repayments by leveraging the continuous yield generated by Mites, ensuring seamless and trustless transactions.

Fan trust pledges to unlock content

Creators can build direct relationships with fans, allowing them to pledge Mites to unlock exclusive content or experiences, fostering a loyal community.

Decentralized scholarships based on epoch-weighted reputation

Implement fair and transparent scholarship programs where eligibility is determined by a decentralized, epoch-weighted reputation system, rewarding consistent participation and trust.

DAOs that reward duration, not noise

Design decentralized autonomous organizations (DAOs) where governance power and rewards are proportional to the duration of token holding, promoting long-term commitment over short-term speculation.

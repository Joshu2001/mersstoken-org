What is Merss?

Merss (MSZ) is a decentralized crypto token designed to make lending, borrowing, and earning rewards radically simpler and more flexible. No banks, no middlemen, and no complex setup just crypto working for you.

By holding MSZ, you earn time-based rewards that grow the longer you stay. You can even use your future yield as collateral to borrow, spend, or support others all without locking or giving up your tokens.

With delegated yield, you can transfer earnings not ownership—making it easy to reward friends, fund DAOs, or empower communities. And since your MSZ remains accessible, you can use it while it earns for you.

Fast, secure, and environmentally conscious, Merss is more than a token, it's a new financial layer for creators, believers, and builders shaping a fairer future.

💬 FAQs

Q: Do I have to stake or farm my MSZ to earn? ▼

A: No. Just hold MSZ in your wallet. The protocol automatically tracks how long you've held it—no staking or farming required.

Q: Can I withdraw rewards anytime? ▼

A: You can withdraw after your Trust Epoch has matured. No penalties, no slashing, just rewards based on real holding time.

Q: Will there be fixed interest rates or APY? ▼

A: Merss does not offer fixed interest rates. Instead, it uses a trust-based system that defines a base annual yield (currently 10%) and adds bonuses for users in higher trust tiers. Your rewards increase based on how long and consistently you hold MSZ.

Q: Can I borrow against my MSZ in the future? ▼

A: Yes. Merss will support borrowing using your Trust Epochs and yield rights as collateral without selling your tokens.

Q: Is my MSZ locked while I earn rewards? ▼

A: No. You can use or transfer your MSZ anytime. Merss tracks holding duration in the background, no lockups required.

Q: What happens if I move my MSZ to another wallet? ▼

A: Moving MSZ to another wallet resets your Trust Epoch in that wallet. To keep your yield progress, hold MSZ in the same wallet.

Q: Can I delegate my yield to multiple wallets? ▼

A: Yes. Merss supports delegated yield via the DYL, allowing you to stream or split rewards to multiple wallets or contracts.

Q: Can I lose my MSZ or rewards if I go inactive? ▼

A: No. Merss is slashing-free. Your rewards and tokens remain intact even if you don’t interact frequently.

Q: Is my yield guaranteed? ▼

A:No. Yield is not guaranteed and depends on how long and how consistently you hold MSZ. The protocol currently defines a base annual rate of 10%, with optional bonus tiers for high-trust users. Your actual return may be lower depending on your holding time and consistency. All rates are subject to change by the DAO.

Q: What is a bonus tier and how do I qualify? ▼

A: Bonus tiers are additional yield boosts awarded to long-term holders or high-trust participants. The more consistently you hold or delegate MSZ, the more likely you are to qualify for higher yield multipliers.

Q: Can I trade or sell my future yield? ▼

A: Planned features will allow you to trade or lease your delegated yield rights on secondary markets—turning time into a tradable asset.

Q: How is Merss different from platforms like Aave or Nexo? ▼

A: Merss doesn’t require staking, inflation, or centralized custody. You earn yield by holding, not locking. You can borrow using your future yield, and delegate it without giving up your tokens. It's a fundamentally different model based on time and trust, not speculation.

Q: Why does time matter in Merss? ▼

A: Time is your proof of trust. The longer and more consistently you hold or delegate MSZ, the more yield and governance power you unlock without slashing or inflation.
